About Us

Welcome to FUREVERHOMES, At FUREVERHOMES, we're passionate about finding forever homes for dogs in need. Our mission is to provide a safe, supportive, and informative platform for dog lovers to adopt, learn, and connect.

Our Story FUREVERHOMES was founded by SAROJINI BOSE AND SHUBHI SINGH in 2024, driven by a desire to make a positive impact on the lives of dogs and their human families. With a team of dedicated animal lovers, we've created a comprehensive online platform that simplifies the dog adoption process and promotes education, awareness, and community engagement.

Our Values - Compassion: We believe that every dog deserves a loving home and a second chance at happiness. - Integrity: We're committed to transparency, honesty, and fairness in all our interactions. - Education: We strive to provide accurate, helpful, and engaging content that informs and inspires dog lovers. - Community: We foster a supportive online community that connects dog adopters, fosters, volunteers, and advocates.
